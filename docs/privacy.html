Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 7, 2024

Introduction

CardConnect ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your email address and display name to create your account.
  • Business Card Data: The contact information you scan and store, including names, phone numbers, emails, company names, and addresses.
  • Card Images: Photos of business cards you choose to save within the app.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the CardConnect service
  • Sync your contacts across your devices
  • Process business card images using AI to extract contact information
  • Improve our AI and services

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase, Google's enterprise-grade cloud platform. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication via Google and Apple Sign-In
  • User-specific data isolation

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication and cloud data storage
  • Google Gemini AI: For business card text extraction and enrichment
  • Apple StoreKit: For subscription management

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as you maintain an active account. You can delete your data at any time by removing cards from the app or contacting us to delete your account entirely.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your contacts
  • Opt out of data processing by uninstalling the app

Children's Privacy

CardConnect is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: cardconnect.support@gmail.com
